This extension provides tibetan spellchecking at syllable
level (not composed words).

The syllables come from the Bod rgya tshik mdzod chen mo
dictionnary, with sanskrit syllables removed.

Xavier Faure <suizokukan@orange.fr> and Elie Roux
<elie.roux@telecom-bretagne.eu> contributed to this package.

You can file bugreports at https://github.com/eroux/tibetan-spellchecker/issues
